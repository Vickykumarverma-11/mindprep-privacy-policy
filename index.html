Privacy Policy for MindPrep

Last updated: February 06, 2026

Introduction

MindPrep ("we", "our", or "us") operates the MindPrep mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

1. Account Information

  • Email address
  • Name (if provided)
  • Academic goal (School, College, or Competitive Exam)
  • Selected subjects

2. Authentication Data

We use Firebase Authentication to manage user accounts. If you sign in with Google, we receive your Google profile information (name and email) as authorized by you.

3. User-Generated Content

  • PDF documents and images you upload for test generation
  • Test responses and scores
  • Test history and performance data

4. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (device model, operating system version)
  • App usage data (features used, session duration)
  • Crash reports and performance data

How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Generate AI-powered MCQ tests from uploaded content
  • Track test scores and performance history
  • Improve functionality and user experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Send important service-related notifications

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore, Firebase Storage, Firebase Authentication). All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).

Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase — Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In — Google Privacy Policy

Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.

Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Update your profile
  • Delete your account
  • Export test history
  • Opt out of optional data collection

Children's Privacy

MindPrep is designed for students of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

Advertising

MindPrep does not display any third-party advertisements. We do not use AdMob or any advertising SDK.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
